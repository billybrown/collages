Collages

After a five year lull completely void of art making, the priorities with my recent studio practice have been simple by necessity; remove any and all hurdles to creating work. My collages are made from magazines sent to my apartment - a free, readily accessible material to use as a raw library of textures, pattern and color. The resulting abstract, active floating shapes have come from experimenting and exploring this material.
